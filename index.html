CT Technology Evolution

Key Milestones
1971 Sir Godfrey Hounsfield conceptualizes the first CT scanner at EMI Laboratories
1972 First successful brain CT scan performed
1973 Commercial release of the first EMI-Scanner
1975 Introduction of whole-body CT scanning
1979 Hounsfield and Cormack win Nobel Prize for CT technology
1983 First 3D reconstructions developed by GE Healthcare
1991 Introduction of Spiral (Helical) CT by Siemens
1998 Launch of Multislice CT (MDCT) by GE and Siemens
2000 Advanced cardiac CT scanning becomes possible
2002 Dual-Energy CT introduced by Philips
2010 AI integration begins in CT scanning technologies
2012 Low-Dose CT technologies developed
2014 Photon-counting CT detectors introduced by Siemens
2020 CT imaging becomes crucial in COVID-19 diagnosis
